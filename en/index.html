About iStarTec

Technical Installation Specialists

iStarTec is a technical installation contractor based in the Algarve, specialising exclusively in hydraulic and plumbing systems for residential construction. We work with construction companies, developers, architects and villa builders throughout the region.

Unlike a general plumbing contractor, our focus is entirely on new construction — integrating systems at the right phase of a build, working from professional drawings, and delivering commissioning documentation at handover.

Our work spans single private residences to multi-unit developments, with a strong track record in Portimão, Albufeira and the wider Algarve coast. We are proud partners of Ordem Geométrica, Tacu Bacaliuc and emohb, collaborating on many of their residential construction projects.
