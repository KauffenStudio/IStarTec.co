Plumbing & Hydraulic Installations in Faro

Hydraulic installations for residential construction in Faro and the eastern Algarve. Integrated systems of plumbing, solar thermal and heat pumps for developers and contractors.

iStarTec in Faro

Faro is the capital of the Algarve and has a different demand profile from the western Algarve — greater diversity of building types (apartments, villas, mixed-use) and a more constant construction pace throughout the year. Our installations in Faro adapt to this variety: from multi-unit residential buildings to private villas, with systems meeting the increasingly strict energy efficiency regulations for new construction.

Our services in Faro

Complete hydraulic installations adapted to the technical profile of each project in Faro — plumbing, DHW, solar thermal systems and heat pumps.

Plumbing Installations

Water supply networks (multilayer, PEX, copper), sewage systems, internal rainwater drainage and ventilation for residential buildings and villas in Faro.

Solar Thermal Systems

Bosch and BAXI solar thermal collectors for DHW, sized for the high solar irradiance of the Algarve. Compliant with energy efficiency regulations.

Underfloor Heating

Hydraulic underfloor heating system with Uponor PEX piping, zone manifolds and thermostatic control. Integration with air-source heat pump.

Bathroom Installations

Supply and installation of premium bathroom equipment for villas and developments in Faro.
