Frequently Asked Questions

Does iStarTec carry out natural gas works?

Not directly. For natural gas installations we subcontract certified companies with category 9 licence and ITG/Floene certification, ensuring full regulatory compliance.

Does iStarTec carry out roadworks and urban infrastructure?

No. Our scope is internal building plumbing and connections to the public branch. We do not undertake public collectors, urban gas networks or road resurfacing.

What is iStarTec's licence?

iStarTec operates with IMPIC licence category 4 class 3 (electrical and mechanical installations up to ~€664,000 per contract). We are qualified to bid on any individual lot within this limit.

Where in the Algarve do you work?

We serve the whole Algarve — Portimão, Albufeira, Lagos, Lagoa, Silves, Faro and Loulé. We have strong experience in residential projects in Praia da Rocha, Alvor and Albufeira Garden.

Do you work with construction companies or only with private clients?

Our main clients are construction companies, property developers, architects and engineering practices. We also undertake projects on private luxury villas and high-end refurbishments.
