iStarTec in Portimão

Portimão is the base for a significant part of our project history. We work regularly with construction firm Ordem Geométrica on multi-unit residential developments and with developers on luxury villas in Praia da Rocha and Alvor. We understand the local technical requirements — sea-air exposure, local regulations, and the need for coordination with architectural practices active in the region.

Our services in Portimão

Complete hydraulic installations, from design to commissioning. We cover internal plumbing, solar thermal DHW and heat pump systems, and premium bathroom installations for clients in Portimão and surroundings.

Plumbing Installations

Water supply networks (multilayer, PEX, copper), sewage systems, internal rainwater drainage and ventilation for residential buildings and villas in Portimão.

Solar Thermal Systems

Underfloor Heating

Hydraulic underfloor heating system with Uponor PEX piping, zone manifolds and thermostatic control. Integration with air-source heat pump.

Frequently Asked Questions — Portimão

What is the typical project scope in Portimão?

Most of our work in Portimão is on multi-unit residential developments (15 to 76 apartments) and detached villas with high technical demands. We cover from Praia da Rocha to more residential areas such as Alvor.

Do you work with Ordem Geométrica?

Yes. iStarTec carries out hydraulic installations regularly with Ordem Geométrica on developments in the Portimão and Praia da Rocha area.

How long does a full installation take for a villa in Portimão?

It depends on size and complexity — typical villas take 6 to 12 weeks for complete plumbing, DHW and underfloor heating installation. Larger developments can take several months, phased with the construction programme.

Do you carry out plumbing refurbishments in Portimão?

Yes. In addition to new builds, we carry out plumbing refurbishments on existing villas and apartments, including replacing old piping with modern systems (multilayer, PEX).

Have a project in Portimão?

We work with construction companies, developers and architects in Portimão and across the Algarve.
