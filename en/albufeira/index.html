Frequently Asked Questions — Albufeira

Do you work with Grupo Libertas in Albufeira?

Yes. We delivered all hydraulic installations at Albufeira Garden — Grupo Libertas' 76-apartment development. Bosch air-source heat pump systems for DHW, solar thermal and full internal plumbing.

What is the typical project size in Albufeira?

Our experience in Albufeira is focused on medium and large developments (50+ apartments) with institutional developers. We also undertake premium detached villas when the client is a developer we already work with.

How do you manage coordination with the contractor on large projects?

We assign a lead technician who interfaces with the contractor throughout the project — from initial hydraulic planning to commissioning and handover. Weekly coordination meetings and flexibility on the installation sequence.
